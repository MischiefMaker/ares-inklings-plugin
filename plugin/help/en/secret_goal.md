# Secret & Goal

**`+inkling/secret <title>/<description>`** - Set your character's secret

**`+inkling/goal <title>/<description>`** - Set your character's goal

**`+inkling/view-secret`**, **`+inkling/view-goal`** - View your current draft (no arguments needed)

You can use the `secret`/`goal` commands repeatedly to edit and refine your secret and goal before your character is approved. Each time you run the command, it will overwrite your previous draft, allowing you to adjust your choices as you develop your character.

When your character is approved, these become real Inklings that you can edit and submit to staff.

For more information about the Inklings system, see `help inklings`.
